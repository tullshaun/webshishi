Our Story

Crafting sustainable luxury since 2020

Our Journey

Founded in 2020, SHISHIBAGS began with a simple vision: to create timeless, sustainable luxury accessories that complement the modern lifestyle. Our journey started in a small workshop in Shanghai, where our founder's passion for traditional craftsmanship met contemporary design.

Today, we continue to honor these roots while embracing innovation and sustainability in every piece we create.

Our Values

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly materials and ethical production practices in everything we create.

Quality

Each piece is crafted with meticulous attention to detail and built to last for generations.

Community

We believe in fostering relationships with our artisans, customers, and communities.
